About Vanguard

Mission and Target Demographics

Our Mission

At Vanguard AI Prep, our mission is to democratize high-level defence exam preparation. By leveraging cutting-edge deterministic AI models, we eliminate the need for exorbitant coaching fees and provide a standardized, highly rigorous practice environment accessible to every ambitious aspirant.

Target Authorization

  • NDA (National Defence Academy): Foundational preparation and Services Selection Board (SSB) interviews module.
  • CDS (Combined Defence Services): High-resolution current affairs, English, and General Knowledge simulation.
  • AFCAT (Air Force Common Admission Test): Precision practice limits for reasoning and deterministic modeling.
  • CAPF (Central Armed Police Forces): Specialized subjective mock testing and structured evaluation loops.
